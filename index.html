The Rec App

The Rec app connects users to established recreational organizations. They will be able to choose the activity they are looking for and company profiles will show up with contact information, the programs they offer, locations and times will be on display quick and efficiently. People will be able to find the activities they know and love faster as well as discover new recreational activities they are interested in. Organizers can make there own profiles showing what they have to offer and connecting to users internally or externally using there contact information. Ultimatly the goal of the Rec app is to make activity easy,accesible, efficient and organized for users so they don't need to go anywhere else to find the recreational activities they enjoy.
